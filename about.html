Established in the 1990s, Azad Provision Store has been the heartbeat of Ambavadi, Ahmedabad. We started as a small family-run corner shop with a simple mission: to provide our community with the freshest groceries and daily essentials at honest, fair prices.

Decades later, we are still family-owned, still treating every customer like a guest in our home, and continually evolving to serve you better — now with Home Delivery and easy WhatsApp ordering.

Whether you need Amul Butter for your morning toast, fresh masalas for Sunday dal, or household essentials at the end of a busy day — Azad Provision Store is always here for you.
